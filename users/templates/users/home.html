About WildWatch

WildWatch is a community-driven platform dedicated to protecting animals and their habitats. By reporting incidents, tracking updates, and contributing as a helper, you can actively participate in safeguarding wildlife. Our platform bridges the gap between those who observe, those who act, and those who care. Whether you're an animal lover, a volunteer, or just someone who wants to make a difference, WildWatch empowers you to take action.

Together, we can raise awareness, help injured animals, and create a safer environment for wildlife. Join us and become a part of a community that cares.

How It Works

Submit a Report

Share details about an animal-related incident you have witnessed. Whether it's an injured animal, a sighting of endangered species, or illegal activities, your report helps us take action.

Track Updates

Stay informed about the progress of your submitted reports. Get notified when helpers are assigned or when the situation is resolved.

Make a Difference

Join the WildWatch community and help animals in need. Contribute as a volunteer, provide assistance, or simply spread awareness to make a positive impact.

Join the WildWatch Community

Become part of a passionate community dedicated to protecting wildlife. By signing up, you can submit reports, track updates, and join forces with others to make a difference. Every voice counts, and together, we can create a better future for our planet's creatures.

Whether you want to contribute actively or simply stay informed, WildWatch welcomes everyone who cares about animals. Take the first step today!
